Our Origins


JSOProductions, previously known as "Jack Studio Official," underwent a drastic transformation from what it originally was. Initially designed by a student in seventh grade at school, the website's purpose was to serve as a way to help a kid deal with his beardom.

Over time, this kid slowly lost interest in his website. However, over a year later, in his IST class, his interest sparked, and his curiosity grew once again. The transformation of JSOProductions was far from effortless as he began making an entirely new webpage, but this time with the help of his friend. Together, they pushed their way through the struggles to transform the once withered website into its new form.

Throughout their progress, they discovered new capabilities with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, such as remembering data, switching visual modes, and more. Today, JSOProductions stands as an achievement as to what they were able to design and create. It demonstrates the capabilities of technologies and these young talented boys.

Thanks to their endless effort on this website, it led them to achieving 100% on their IST assessment.
